The data boulangerie for NUS

Feedbaker is the brainchild of Team Jonathans,
and is a summer project for the NUS Orbital Programme of 2014.

Feedbaker is a lightweight web application that allows users to create and share polls for all good-natured purposes and effectively eliminates the need for the responder device which is costly to replace. Students and staff alike thus have alleviated worries about the operational and logistical concerns of said device.
